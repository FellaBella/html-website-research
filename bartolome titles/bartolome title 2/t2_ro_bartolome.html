COMPARATIVE EVALUATION ON THE DURABILITY OF OPTIMIZED EARTHENWARE CLAY-LATEX GLAZED POTS AND COMMERCIAL CLAY POTS

RESEARCH OBJECTIVES

To evaluate the durability and performance of optimized earthenware clay composite
garden pots that are glazed with latex paint in comparison to traditional clay pots by
analyzing their physical, mechanical, and environmental properties under various conditions,
and to determine their feasibility and sustainability as an alternative gardening solution.
