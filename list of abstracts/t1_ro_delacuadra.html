  • This study aims to determine how hydrodynamic factors such as flow velocity, water depth, and shear stress influence the nest site selection of janitor fish in the Marikina River, while also identifying which bank and substrate characteristics, including grain size, cohesion, and slope, are associated with nesting locations.
