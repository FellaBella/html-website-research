Robo-Onion: An Innovative Sensor-based Forecasting System for Optimizing Allium cepa L. (Onion) Growth and Yield

RESULTS

Robo-Onion has succesfully monitored all the growth and yield factors of Allium cepa L., which include plant density, plant spacing, amount of water in the crops, temperature, and nutritional level.

Results of the paired t-test revealed statistically significant differences between sensor readings and manual measurements for temperature, amount of water, nutrional level, plant spacing, and plant density.

Accuracy assessment using percentage error indicated that the Robo-Onion sensors achieved an average accuracy rate of over 90% across all measured parameters. Response time analysis demonstrated that the system detected changes in environmental conditions within a short time frame, allowing timely data updates suitable for field monitoring.

Overall, the results indicate that Robo-Onion is a reliable and effective environmental monitoring tool capable of supporting rice farmers in making informed decisions related to the growth and yield factors
