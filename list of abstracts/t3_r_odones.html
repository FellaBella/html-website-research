RESULTS

The biodegrable temperature-responsive wristbands are able to change their colors in the degrees the leuco-dyes were set to.

Results of the waterload tests, thermometer, and one-way ANOVA revealed statistically accurate measures and were able to pass as a wearable wristband.

Overall, the results indicate that the temperature-responsive biodegrable wristbands are a reliable and effective heat detecting tool capable of supporting individuals who are vulnerable to high heat levels
