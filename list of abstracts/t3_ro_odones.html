Development of Biodegradable Temperature-Responsive Wristbands Using Starch Films and Microencapsulated Thermochromic Leuco-dyes for Detecting Heat

OBJECTIVES OF THE STUDY

  1. To develop a biodegrable, temperature-responsive wristband using starch films and microencapsulated thermochromic leuco-dyes for the purpose of detecting dangerous heat levels.
  2. To reduce risks related to heat exposure for individuals that react dangerously to a higher heat index.
