Development of Biodegradable Temperature-Responsive Wristbands Using Starch Films and Microencapsulated Thermochromic Leuco-dyes for Detecting Heat

STATEMENT OF THE PROBLEM

  1. Individuals react differently to certain heat levels, the creation of temperature-change visual signals can reduce risks that come with heat exposure.
  2. Electronic devices, such as smart watches create e-waste, unlike temperature-responsive bioodegrable wristbands.
