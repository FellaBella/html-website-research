AgroSentinel: A Hybrid Bio-Robotic and Environmental Sensing System for Enhancing Typhoon Resilience in Rice Production

OBJECTIVES OF THE STUDY

  1. To develop an Arduino-based environmental monitoring system for rice farming.
  2. To monitor temperature, humidity, soil moisture, and rainfall in real time.
  3. To help rice farmers prepare for and respond to typhoons using timely environmental data.
  4. To test the accuracy and reliability of the system under different weather conditions.
  5. To reduce crop losses and improve rice production through better monitoring and decision-making.
