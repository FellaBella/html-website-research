Waste Management Arduino-Based Robot Kinematics that Utilize OV7607 Camera Sensor and HC-SR04 Ultrasonic Sensor

RESULTS (EXPECTED RESULT IN THE FUTURE)

The study aims to create a completely functional and sturdy robot that can collect different types of garbage. This study is also expected to contribute to the existing knowledge in the field of robotics by increasing information about the field; and lastly, the researchers also expect that this study will help the community in making the environment cleaner.
